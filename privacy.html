Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

FAB Apps ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Tora Study mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address — collected during registration or via Google Sign-In.
  • Name and profile picture — obtained from Google OAuth when you sign in with Google.
  • Device ID — an anonymous identifier used to prevent abuse of the sign-up credit bonus.
  • Usage data — queries sent to the AI (chat, quiz, summary).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication and account management.
  • Managing your credit balance for AI features.
  • Generating AI-powered responses via OpenAI.
  • Processing in-app purchases via Google Play Billing and RevenueCat.
  • Improving and maintaining the App.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google OAuth / Google Play — for authentication and in-app purchases.
  • OpenAI — to power AI features.
  • RevenueCat — for managing and verifying in-app purchases.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and hashed passwords.

6. Your Rights — Account Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. feedback@fab-apps.com.

Upon deletion, we remove your email, name, picture, credit balance, transaction history, quiz sessions, reading plans and notes.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at feedback@fab-apps.com.
